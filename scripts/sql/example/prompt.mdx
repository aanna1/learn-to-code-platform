Write a query that returns the **name and price of the three most expensive
products**, with the priciest first.

The `products` table is already seeded for you:

| id | name  | price | category |
|----|-------|-------|----------|
| 1  | Lamp  | 40    | home     |
| 2  | Chair | 95    | home     |
| 3  | Desk  | 210   | home     |
| 4  | Pen   | 3     | office   |
| 5  | Mug   | 12    | office   |

Return only the `name` and `price` columns, sorted from most to least expensive,
and keep just the top three. Press **Run** to see your result set, then **Submit**
to check it against the hidden tests.
